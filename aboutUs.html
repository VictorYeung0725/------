STORY

EVA is committed to bringing the best local and overseas photography collections to the audience, and also provides exposure opportunities for potential new-generation art talents, so that the field of photography art in Hong Kong can continue to develop in the long run.
We rely on everyone's support so that we can continue to provide audiences with high-quality works and services and continue to cultivate more young artists, so as to create a rich, diverse and vibrant cultural scene for Hong Kong.

EVA致力為觀眾帶來本地及海外最優質的攝影集，亦為一眾有潛質的新生代藝術人才提供曝光機會，讓香港攝影藝術領域得以長遠持續發展。
我們有賴每位的支持，讓我們繼續為觀眾和提供優質的作品及服務，並持續栽培更多年青藝術家，為香港打造一個豐富多元及充滿活力的文化新景象
